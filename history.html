Our Sacred Journey

From humble beginnings in 1996 to becoming a beacon of faith and hope, our journey reflects God's faithfulness through every season of growth and challenge.

Timeline of Faith

1996

The Foundation

Bishop William Laktari founded Gospel Springs with a vision to create a community rooted in faith. Our first service was held in a small rented hall with just 20 faithful members.

2001

First Sanctuary

After years of growth, we built our first permanent sanctuary, a testament to God's provision and the dedication of our members who sacrificed to make this dream a reality.

2005

Community Outreach

Launched our first major outreach program, feeding the homeless and supporting local families. This marked the beginning of our commitment to serve beyond our church walls.

2012

Youth Movement

Established our dynamic youth ministry that has since nurtured hundreds of young believers. Our annual youth conference became a regional gathering for spiritual renewal.

2018

Expansion Vision

Acquired land for our current campus, with plans for a worship center, community center, and educational facilities to serve future generations.

2020

Digital Transformation

During the pandemic, we embraced technology to continue worship online, reaching a global audience and discovering new ways to connect and minister.

Key Milestones

3 Locations

From one humble beginning to three thriving locations serving diverse communities across the region.

15+ Ministries

Comprehensive programs serving all ages from children to seniors, meeting spiritual and practical needs.

Global Reach

Our online services and missions work now impact lives across 5 continents through digital platforms.
