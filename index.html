About Our Church

Established in 1990 by Senior Bishop William Laktari, Gospel Springs Mission has been a beacon of hope in Isongo and beyond. From humble beginnings as a home fellowship, we've grown into a vibrant spiritual community impacting lives through dynamic worship, practical teaching, and compassionate outreach.

Founder & Lead Bishop

Bishop William Laktari

Global Ministry Founder since 1990

Bishop William Laktari is the visionary founder of Gospel Springs Mission with over 30 years of apostolic ministry. Based in the United States, he provides strategic leadership while maintaining spiritual oversight of the global ministry.

  • Established Gospel Springs Mission in 1990
  • Planted 14 churches across Kenya

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is your church located?

Our church is located at Isongo .,We also have number of branch churches, We welcome you to join us for worship and fellowship!.Check our"About" page to know more.

What services do you offer?

We offer Sunday worship, Bible studies, youth programs, community outreach, and pastoral counseling.

What are your service times?

Our Sunday services are at 8:00 AM-1:00 PM.Check our"Services"page for more

How can I get involved?

You can join our volunteer teams, small groups, or our community. Visit our "Communities" or "Connect" page or contact us for more information.

Do you have programs for children and youth?

Yes! We have Sunday School, youth groups, and special events for children and teens. Check our "about us>Ministries" page for details.

Do you have scholarship programs for students

Yes, we offer scholarships! These are available for ministry members who are students in primary, secondary, or university. For more details, please visit our "Scholarships" page or contact our office.
