Beloved in Christ,

As we stand in this new season of ministry, my heart overflows with gratitude for your steadfast partnership in the Gospel. For nearly three decades, Gospel Springs Mission has been a vessel of transformation, pouring living water into thirsty souls across our continent.

Our sacred mandate remains unchanged: Winning the lost, Discipling believers, and Sending forth ambassadors of Christ. This threefold mission finds its roots in our Lord's Great Commission:

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit..."
- Matthew 28:19 (ESV)

This year, we're pioneering new strategies for digital discipleship while deepening our roots in community transformation. Our upcoming School of Ministry will equip 500 emerging leaders with tools for spiritual warfare and cross-cultural evangelism.

I urge you to join us in this harvest season through:

  • Prayer covering for our missionaries in high-risk regions
  • Supporting our Bible translation projects
  • Participating in our global prayer chain

The fields are ripe, but the workers are few. Together, we can flood nations with the springs of living water that bring eternal life.

Yours in Christ's Service,
