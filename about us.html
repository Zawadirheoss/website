Our Sacred Journey

Founded in 1996, Gospel Springs Mission International Church began as a small gathering of faithful believers in a humble home. Through divine guidance and unwavering faith, we've grown into a vibrant spiritual family with multiple branches. Our journey reflects God's faithfulness through every challenge and triumph.

From our first baptism in a local river to constructing our current sanctuary, each milestone has been a testament to God's provision. We honor our founders' vision while embracing new generations of believers.

Historical Timeline

1996

Humble Beginnings

Founded with 12 members meeting in the Pastor's living room

1998

Vision for Permanent Home

Began planning and fundraising for a permanent church structure

2000

Building Our Sanctuary

Construction began on our first permanent worship center

2002

First Branch Church

Established our first branch congregation in a neighboring community

2005

Ministry Expansion

Began planting multiple branch churches across the region

2006

Lifespring Academy

Founded our Christian-based educational institution

2010

Outreach Advancement

Expanded our ministry through community outreach programs and missions

Present

Continuing the Mission

Growing our church network and impacting communities through faith and education
