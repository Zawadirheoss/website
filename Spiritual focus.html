"Go and make disciples of all nations"

Matthew 28:19 (KJV)

The Divine Mandate

The Great Commission is not merely a suggestion but Christ's final command to His Church. This imperative call contains four vital elements:

Universal Scope

"All nations" emphasizes global evangelism without cultural or ethnic boundaries

Discipleship Process

Making followers through teaching, baptism, and spiritual mentoring

Trinitarian Foundation

Baptizing in the name of Father, Son, and Holy Spirit

Perpetual Mission

"Until the end of the age" - an ongoing mandate for all believers
